SYSTEM GUIDE

PHP/Web is the Hospital Management System.
It handles hospital-side records:
- patients
- doctors
- appointments
- admissions
- services and charges
- invoice monitoring

C# Windows Forms is the Billing and Invoice System.
It handles billing-side work:
- selecting patients
- selecting hospital services
- adding custom billing items
- calculating subtotal, discount, tax, total, paid amount, and balance
- saving invoices
- viewing invoice records
- updating invoice payment amount

CONNECTION FLOW:

C# Billing System -> PHP API -> SQLite Database

PHP Hospital Management System -> SQLite Database

When the billing staff saves an invoice in C#, the invoice appears in the PHP Hospital Management invoice monitor.
